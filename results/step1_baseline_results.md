## Key Observations

1. **Cora/Citeseer**: Large gap (12-23%), graph structure is crucial, high KD potential
2. **PubMed**: Moderate gap (~5.5%), node features already discriminative
3. **Amazon datasets**: After fixing hyperparameters, GCN achieves SOTA-level performance (90%/94%)
4. **Critical fix**: Amazon datasets require `weight_decay=0` and larger hidden dim (256)
